About ShramConnect

ShramConnect is a platform that connects skilled and unskilled workers with contractors who are looking for workers for construction, plumbing, electrical, driving, cleaning and other jobs. Our goal is to make hiring workers easy, fast and reliable.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide employment opportunities to daily wage workers and help contractors find workers easily in their local area.

Services We Provide

✔ Worker Registration
✔ Contractor Job Posting
✔ Job Search by Skill & Location
✔ Apply for Jobs
✔ View Applications
✔ Find Workers by Location

How It Works

Workers create profile → Search jobs → Apply → Contractor contacts worker. Contractors post jobs → Receive applications → Hire workers.
